Tribute to Linus Torvalds

The Creator of Linux and Git

About Linus Torvalds

Linus Benedict Torvalds is a Finnish-American software engineer who created the Linux kernel, which became the foundation for many operating systems, including the Linux operating system and Android. He is also the creator of Git, a distributed version control system widely used in software development.

Why Linus Torvalds Inspires Me

I admire Linus Torvalds for his incredible contributions to open-source software. His work on Linux has made technology accessible to millions around the globe, fostering innovation and collaboration. Git, his other revolutionary invention, has become an indispensable tool for developers worldwide.

Legacy

Linus Torvalds' legacy lives on in every Linux-powered device, from smartphones to supercomputers, and in every software repository managed with Git. He is a symbol of the open-source movement and proof that one person's vision can change the world.
